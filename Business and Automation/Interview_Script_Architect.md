The Interview Script Architect

Overview

A structured interviewing system designed to generate behavioral questions that reveal decision-making patterns, judgment, ethics, and problem-solving ability.

The goal is to move beyond rehearsed answers and uncover how candidates think in real workplace situations.

Core Prompt

Create 3 behavioral interview questions for:

[Job Role]

Requirements:

- Focus on real workplace situations
- Include ambiguity or uncertainty
- Explore decision-making processes
- Test ethical judgment
- Avoid generic interview questions

For each question, identify:

- Competency Being Evaluated
- Behavioral Question



Example Output

Input

Product Manager

Output

Competency: Decision Making

Tell me about a time when you had to choose between shipping a feature quickly and addressing known customer concerns. How did you make the decision?
