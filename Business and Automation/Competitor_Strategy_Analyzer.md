Competitor Strategy Analyzer

Overview

A market intelligence system designed to uncover unmet customer needs by analyzing competitor reviews, complaints, and feedback patterns.

The goal is to identify opportunities competitors are overlooking and reveal areas where products, services, or messaging can be improved.

Core Prompt

Analyze these competitor reviews:

[Reviews]

Requirements:

- Identify recurring complaints
- Identify recurring requests
- Highlight underserved customer needs
- Separate surface complaints from root causes
- Suggest strategic opportunities

Output:

1. Key Customer Complaints
2. Unmet Customer Needs
3. Market Gaps
4. Strategic Opportunities



Example Output

Input

Competitor reviews mentioning slow onboarding, confusing dashboards, and poor customer support.

Output

Market Gap:

Customers want simplicity more than additional features.

Strategic Opportunity:

Position the product around ease of adoption and faster time-to-value.
